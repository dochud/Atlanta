Lincoln + Vietnam + Korea Memorials

DC Practical Tips
💡
Getting Around

Metro — Clean, safe, simple. SmarTrip works like Oyster. ~$2–7 per journey. Silver Line is your Dulles connection.

Walking — The Mall is deceptively long. Allow 15–20 min between monuments. Comfortable shoes are non-negotiable.

Uber/Lyft — Fine for evenings or when feet give out. Cheaper than NYC.

🌡️
Late May Weather

Expect 22–28°C and humid. Comfortable in the morning, sticky by afternoon. Sunscreen, water bottle, comfortable shoes essential for Mall days. Evenings are pleasant — ideal for the monuments walk.
