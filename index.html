The National Mall Monuments
Walk west from White House · ~2 hours at a relaxed pace

Washington Monument — worth the base visit even without going up. Book inside at recreation.gov.

WWII Memorial — 5 min west. Fountains, state pillars. Often missed, genuinely moving.

Lincoln Memorial — west along the Reflecting Pool. One of the great public spaces in the world.

Vietnam Veterans Memorial — immediately right of Lincoln. 58,000 names on black granite. The most affecting thing in DC.

Korea War Veterans Memorial — 5 min south of Lincoln. Ghostly steel soldiers in a field.

🌡️ Late May Weather
What to Expect

22–28°C and humid. Comfortable mornings, sticky afternoons. Sunscreen, water bottle, and comfortable shoes are non-negotiable on Mall days. Evenings are pleasant.

💡 Good to Know
DC Practicalities

Tipping — 18–20% at restaurants. Bars slightly less.

Currency — Cards accepted everywhere. Notify your bank.

Smithsonian — All museums free. No dress code.
